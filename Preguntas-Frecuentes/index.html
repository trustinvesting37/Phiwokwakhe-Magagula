How does the company generate the profits to be distributed from the marketing plan?

60% is destined to work in the markets where the company has contracted the service of professional traders through legal contract. That money is distributed to invest in lucrative markets such as Stock market, trading and arbitrage in Cryptocurrencies. At the end of the day the company receives the profits for the investments made by the traders and distributes a % or daily fee to each investor. It also allocates part of those earnings for a financial cushion. In case of not having obtained profits, ensure the payment to the affiliates, thanks to the historical accumulation of the financial buffer. and 40% destined to the marketing plan. That is why the bonuses are available every day in available (of each contracted service).
